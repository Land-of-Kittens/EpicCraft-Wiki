        The Super-High Speed Iceways, often referred to as simply Iceways, is a series of tunnels connecting the majority of towns, communities and player bases.

        It's design allows for boat usage with lower frictional resistance than walking, riding horses or sailing, and greater speed than railways. It is often the preferred method of high-distance traveling since they don't require the use any kind of fuel, unlike elytra.

        The majority of the iceways are located in the Overworld, although currently a single iceway exists on top of the Nether dimension.

        Infrastructure

        The Overworld

        The iceways are commonly built at y=40 and excavated in a 4-wide 3-tall manner, with torches often placed for lighting. At later stages, the lower part is filled with a checkered pattern of packed ice and air due to the low-resistance high-speed boat usage this design presents.

        If the tunnels enter an underwater section, sometimes glass is placed to enclose the railway instead of other non-transparent blocks.

        When entering a station, the floor is replaced with solid blocks patterns, which greatly lowers the speed to greatly improve maneuverability.

        The Nether

        The single nether iceway, situated in the roof of the dimension, consists of the regular packed ice and air pattern with cobblestone slabs for guard railings. It connects the Weizenburg Nether Portal with a player base situated at 8km away.
