        Jeder Station is a terminus for both directions of Weizenburg Local Railway. Currently it is the only overground station on the line.

        Contents

        • 1 Overview
        • 2 Services
          • 2.1 Weizenburg Local Railway
        • 3 Gallery

        Overview

        Situated in mostly flat, southern area of Weizenburg, it is a neighbouring building of Jeder's House, and their storage and smelter buildings. There is a path connecting the area with the rest of the town.

        The station features four dead-end platforms, two of which are terminating.

        Services

        Weizenburg Local Railway

        Clockwise, towards Tuxcrafting Station

        Anticlockwise, towards Hellenion Station
